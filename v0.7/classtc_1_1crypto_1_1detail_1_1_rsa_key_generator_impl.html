Generate an RSA key.

Parameters
[in]key_bit_sizeSize of rsa key in bits.
[out]nBuffer to store modulus.
[in]n_sizeSize of modulus buffer.
[out]pBuffer to store prime p.
[in]p_sizeSize of prime p buffer.
[out]qBuffer to store prime q.
[in]q_sizeSize of prime q buffer.
[out]dBuffer to store private exponent.
[in]d_sizeSize of private exponent buffer.
[out]eBuffer to store public exponent.
[in]e_sizeSize of public exponent buffer.
Precondition
  • key_bit_size must a multiple of 8 bits (byte aligned).
Postcondition
  • Key components are exported if the related buffers were not null.
Note
  • Key components can be optionally not exported if the corresponding input variables are null and zero.
